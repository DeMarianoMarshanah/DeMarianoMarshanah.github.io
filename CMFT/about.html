MADDIE MADAYAG

  • Madeleine Yrenea "Maddie" Madayag (born February 7, 1998) is a Filipino volleyball player who currently plays for a local club team named Choco Mucho Flying Titans. She was a member of the collegiate varsity women's volleyball team of Ateneo de Manila University. She is a current member of Philippines national team.

  • height- 5 ft 11

BEA DE LEON

  • Isabel Beatriz "Bea" Paras de Leon (born August 2, 1996) is a Filipino professional volleyball player who currently plays for the club team Choco Mucho Flying Titans in the PVL. She played for the Ateneo Lady Eagles in the UAAP for five straight years and on her final year, led her team to its third championship and was named the UAAP Season 81 Finals MVP.
  • Height-181cm (5 ft 11 in)
  • College: ADMU
  • Possition- Middle Blocker

DEANNA WONG

  • Maria Deanna Izabella Alvizo Wong (July 18, 1998) is a Filipino Chinese volleyball athlete from Cebu, Philippines. She has been a member of the Ateneo de Manila University Lady Eagles, the school's senior women's volleyball team, since her rookie year in 2016. During Season 80 of the University Athletic Association of the Philippines (UAAP) in 2018, she won the Best Setter Award. Wong played in Season 4 Open Conference with Choco Mucho Flying Titans, the team owned by Republic Biscuit Corporation.
  • Wong was also part of the Philippine women's national team which competed as the club side, Team Choco Mucho at the 2021 Asian Women's Club Volleyball Championship.>
  • Height - 1.73m (5 ft 8 in)
  • Position - Setter
